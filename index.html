Connect to Arduino

Plug the tablet into the Arduino Uno via a data-capable USB OTG cable, then tap Connect and select the Arduino's serial port from the browser prompt.

Rotation (Motor 1)

Positive = clockwise, negative = counter-clockwise. Spins continuously until Emergency Stop is pressed.

Linear Axis (Motor 2)

Positive = forward, negative = backward. Range −100000 to +100000 steps.
